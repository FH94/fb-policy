# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how our application (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects information obtained through Meta Platforms (Facebook and Instagram).

## Information We Collect
We collect only the data submitted through Facebook Lead Ads forms, such as name, email address, phone number, or any other fields included in the lead form.

## How We Use the Information
The data is used solely for the purpose of:
- Creating or updating leads in our CRM system (e.g., Odoo)
- Contacting the individual who submitted the form regarding their inquiry
- Measuring and improving lead quality

We do **not** share, sell, or use the information for any advertising or unrelated marketing purposes.

## Data Storage and Security
All data is securely transmitted and stored within our CRM or automation systems. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.

## Data Retention
We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill its intended purpose or comply with applicable regulations.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **support@yourdomain.com**.

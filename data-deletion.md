# User Data Deletion Instructions

If you have used our application and wish to delete your data collected through Facebook Lead Ads, you can request deletion by emailing us at **flsm360@gmail.com**.

Please include:
- Your full name
- The email or phone number used in the Facebook form
- The Page or form you submitted the lead through

Upon receiving your request, we will delete your information from our systems within 7 business days.
